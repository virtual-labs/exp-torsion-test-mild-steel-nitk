TORSION TEST ON MILD STEEL

Objective

To study the mechanical properties of mild steel under torsion.

Apparatus used:

Torsion testing machine,Troptometer,Micrometer,Scale.

STEP  1 

 Measure the Initial Diameter of the torsion test sample at two or three places and the average value is obtained.

Torsion Test Sample

STEP    2 

 The gauge length of the beam is restricted at 180mm.

STEP    3 

 Now the specimen is chalked out on the gauge length for understanding the effect of torsion.

STEP   4 

 Insert the specimen into its position within the torsional experimental setup.

STEP   5 

 Start the motor and slowly apply the twisting load on the specimen.
